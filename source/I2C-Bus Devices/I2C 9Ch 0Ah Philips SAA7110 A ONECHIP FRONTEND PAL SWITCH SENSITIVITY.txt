I2C 9Ch/0Ah - Philips SAA7110(A) ONE-CHIP FRONT-END - PAL SWITCH SENSITIVITY
Access:	Write-Only
Notes:	Valid values - FFh = LOW, 80h = MEDIUM, 00h = HIGH
	HIGH means immediate sequence correction.
SeeAlso: I2C 9Eh,I2C 9Ch/0Bh
