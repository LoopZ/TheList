INT 2F - QRIP/TSR - HANGUP
	AX = AC19h
	DI = 1092h
Return: nothing
Desc:	inform QRIP that carrier has been lost
Note:	if DI <> 1092h on entry, QRIP chains the call
SeeAlso: AX=ACF0h
