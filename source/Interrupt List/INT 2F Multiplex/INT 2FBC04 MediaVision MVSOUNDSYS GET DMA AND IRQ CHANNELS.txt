INT 2F - MediaVision MVSOUND.SYS - GET DMA AND IRQ CHANNELS
	AX = BC04h
Return: AX = 4D56h ('MV')
	BL = DMA channel
	CL = IRQ number
SeeAlso: AX=BC00h/BX=3F3Fh,AX=BC01h"MVSOUND"
