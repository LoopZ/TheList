INT 2F U - DOS 3.0+ internal - FLUSH AND FREE DISK BUFFER
	AX = 1209h
	DS:DI -> disk buffer
Return: disk buffer marked unused, contents written to disk if buffer dirty
Note:	can be called only from within DOS
SeeAlso: AX=120Eh,AX=1215h
