INT E0 - PCROBOTS v1.41 - "DAMAGE" - DETERMINE HOW MUCH DAMAGE SUSTAINED
	AX = 0013h
Return: BX = damage status
Note:	the initial value depends on configuration, but is typically 100; as
	  the robot is damaged, it decreases
