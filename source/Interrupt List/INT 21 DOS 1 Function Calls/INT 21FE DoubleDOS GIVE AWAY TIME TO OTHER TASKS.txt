INT 21 - DoubleDOS - GIVE AWAY TIME TO OTHER TASKS
	AH = FEh
	AL = number of 55ms time slices to give away
Return: returns after giving away time slices
SeeAlso: AH=EEh"DoubleDOS",INT FE"DoubleDOS"
