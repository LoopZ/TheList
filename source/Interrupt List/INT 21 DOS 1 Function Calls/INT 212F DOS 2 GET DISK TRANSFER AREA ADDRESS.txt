INT 21 - DOS 2+ - GET DISK TRANSFER AREA ADDRESS
	AH = 2Fh
Return: ES:BX -> current DTA
Note:	under the FlashTek X-32 DOS extender, the pointer is in ES:EBX
SeeAlso: AH=1Ah
