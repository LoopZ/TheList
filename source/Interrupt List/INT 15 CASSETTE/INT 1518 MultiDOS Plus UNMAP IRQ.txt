INT 15 - MultiDOS Plus - UNMAP IRQ
	AH = 18h
	AL = IRQ to unmap (01h-0Fh)
Return: AX = status
	    0000h successful
	    0001h invalid IRQ
Note:	results are unpredictable if the IRQ has not been mapped
SeeAlso: AH=17h"MultiDOS",AH=19h"MultiDOS"
