INT 15 U - HP 100LX/200LX - SERIAL INTERFACE CONTROL
	AH = 4Ah
	AL = control
	    00h deactivate
	    01h activate
Note:	interface selected with AH=49h will be (de)activated
SeeAlso: AH=49h"HP",AH=4Bh"HP"
