INT 15 - Compaq Systempro - MULTIPROCESSOR END-OF-DISPATCH
	AX = E10Eh
	ES:BX -> start of 2nd processor's execution
Return: AL = status
	    0Fh successful (halted)
	    00h failure (not halted)
SeeAlso: AX=E00Fh,AX=E200h
