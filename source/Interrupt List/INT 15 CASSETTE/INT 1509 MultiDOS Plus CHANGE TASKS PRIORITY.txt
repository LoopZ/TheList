INT 15 - MultiDOS Plus - CHANGE TASK'S PRIORITY
	AH = 09h
	AL = new priority
Return: nothing
Note:	the priority has different meanings depending on whether priority-
	  based or round-robin scheduling is used
SeeAlso: AH=07h"MultiDOS"
