INT 15 - HUNTER 16 - OVERRIDE LOW POWER TURN OFF
	AH = 7Dh
	AL = 00h turn off after 10 warnings
	    else never turn off
Desc:	specify whether the Hunter 16 turns off after 10 low power warnings
SeeAlso: AH=7Ch
