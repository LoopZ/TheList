INT 15 - HUNTER 16 - CONTROL POWER DOWN AVAILABILITY
	AH = 69h
	AL = mode
	    00h inhibit power down
	    01h allow power down
Return: AH = status
SeeAlso: AH=67h,AH=68h,AH=6Ah
