INT 15 - HUNTER 16 - CONTROL SOUND
	AH = 70h
	AL = new state
	    00h disable sound
	    else enable sound
Note:	the Husky Hunter 16 is an 8088-based ruggedized laptop.	 Other family
	  members are the Husky Hunter, Husky Hunter 16/80, and Husky Hawk.
