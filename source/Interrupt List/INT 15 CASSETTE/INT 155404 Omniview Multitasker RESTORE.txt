INT 15 C - Omniview Multitasker - RESTORE
	AX = 5404h
	ES:DX = process swapping in
Note:	called by OmniView to notify programs loaded before OmniView of state
	  changes inside OmniView
SeeAlso: AX=5403h,INT 2F/AX=DE09h
