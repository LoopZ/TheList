INT 15 - Compaq SLT/286 or Portable 386 - READ LCD/PLASMA TIMEOUT
	AX = 4000h
Return: AX = 4000h
	CL = timeout in minutes, 00h if disabled
SeeAlso: AX=4001h,AX=4600h
