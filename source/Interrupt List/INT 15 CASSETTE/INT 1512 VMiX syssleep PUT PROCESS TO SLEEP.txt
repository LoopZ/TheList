INT 15 - VMiX - "sys_sleep" - PUT PROCESS TO SLEEP
	AH = 12h
	STACK:	WORD	process ID
Return: AX = status (SYS_OK or SYS_ERROR)
SeeAlso: AH=03h"MultiDOS",AX=101Dh,AH=13h"VMiX"
