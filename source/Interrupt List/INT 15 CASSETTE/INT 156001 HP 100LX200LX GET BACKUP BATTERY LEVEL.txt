INT 15 U - HP 100LX/200LX - GET BACKUP BATTERY LEVEL
	AX = 6001h
Return: AX = battery level
Note:	multiply the returned value with 1Bh and add 622h to get millivolts
SeeAlso: AX=6000h,AX=6002h
