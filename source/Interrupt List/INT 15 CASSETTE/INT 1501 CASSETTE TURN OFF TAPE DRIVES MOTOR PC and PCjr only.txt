INT 15 - CASSETTE - TURN OFF TAPE DRIVE'S MOTOR (PC and PCjr only)
	AH = 01h
Return: CF set on error
	    AH = 86h no cassette present
	CF clear if successful
SeeAlso: AH=00h"CASSETTE"
