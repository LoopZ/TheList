INT 15 - HP 95LX/100LX/200LX - INSTALLATION CHECK
	AX = 4DD4h
Return: BX = 4850h ("HP") if HP 95LX/100LX/200LX
	    CX = model
		0101h HP 95LX
		0102h HP 100LX/200LX
	    DH = ???
		02h HP 200LX 2MB BIOS 1.01 A D german
	    DL = ???
		00h HP 95LX
		01h HP 200LX 2MB BIOS 1.01 A D german
