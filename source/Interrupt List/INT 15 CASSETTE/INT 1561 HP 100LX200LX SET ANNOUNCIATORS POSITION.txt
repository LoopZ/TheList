INT 15 U - HP 100LX/200LX - SET ANNOUNCIATORS POSITION
	AH = 61h
	AL = position (20h = left, 60h = right)
Note:	The announciators are the indicator symbols for the Shift and Fn keys
	  in the bottom line
SeeAlso: AH=62h"HP",INT 16/AH=02h
