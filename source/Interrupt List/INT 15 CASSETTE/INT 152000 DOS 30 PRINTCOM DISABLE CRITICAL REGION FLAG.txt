INT 15 U - DOS 3.0+ PRINT.COM - DISABLE CRITICAL REGION FLAG
	AX = 2000h
Return: nothing
Desc:	stop setting user flag on entry to PRINT critical region
Note:	also supported by PC Network v1.00 RECEIVER.COM
SeeAlso: AX=2001h
