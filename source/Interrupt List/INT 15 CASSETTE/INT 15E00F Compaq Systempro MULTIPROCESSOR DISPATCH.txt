INT 15 - Compaq Systempro - MULTIPROCESSOR DISPATCH
	AX = E00Fh
	ES:BX -> start of 2nd processor's execution
Return: AL = status
	    0Fh successful
	    00h failure
SeeAlso: AX=E10Eh,AX=E200h
