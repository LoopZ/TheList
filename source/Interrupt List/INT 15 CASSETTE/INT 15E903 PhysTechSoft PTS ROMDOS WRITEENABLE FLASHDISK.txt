INT 15 - PhysTechSoft PTS ROM-DOS - WRITE-ENABLE FLASH-DISK
	AX = E903h
Return: nothing
Note:	Enabling writing on Flash disk can lead to corruption of EMS disk info.
SeeAlso: AX=E904h,AX=E905h
