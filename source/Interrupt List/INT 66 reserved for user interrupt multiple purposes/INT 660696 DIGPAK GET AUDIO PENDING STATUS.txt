INT 66 - DIGPAK - GET AUDIO PENDING STATUS
	AX = 0696h
Return: AX = status
	    0000h no sound is playing
	    0001h sound playing, and a sound is pending
	    0002h sound playing, no sound pending
SeeAlso: AX=0695h
