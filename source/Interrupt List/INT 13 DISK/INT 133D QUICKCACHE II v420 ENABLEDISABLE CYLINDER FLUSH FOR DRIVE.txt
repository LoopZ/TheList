INT 13 U - QUICKCACHE II v4.20 - ENABLE/DISABLE CYLINDER FLUSH FOR DRIVE
	AH = 3Dh
	AL = new state (01h enabled, else disabled)
	DL = drive number (00h = A:)
Return: AX = status (0000h successful)
