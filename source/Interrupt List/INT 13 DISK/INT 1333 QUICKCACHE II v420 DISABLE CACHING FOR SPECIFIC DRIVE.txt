INT 13 U - QUICKCACHE II v4.20 - DISABLE CACHING FOR SPECIFIC DRIVE
	AH = 33h
	AL = drive number (00h=A:)
Return: AX = status (0000h successful)
SeeAlso: AH=22h"QUICKCACHE",AH=32h
