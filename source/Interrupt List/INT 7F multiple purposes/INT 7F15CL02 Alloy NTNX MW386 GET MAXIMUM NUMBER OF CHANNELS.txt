INT 7F - Alloy NTNX, MW386 - GET MAXIMUM NUMBER OF CHANNELS
	AH = 15h
	CL = 02h
Return: AH = number of channels available (40h for MW386)
Note:	the application may always assume at least 32 channels available
SeeAlso: AH=15h/CL=03h
