INT 14 - FOSSIL - EXTENDED ^C/^K CHECKING AND TRANSMIT ON/OFF
	AH = 10h
	AL = bit mask
	    bit 0: enable/disable ^C/^K checking
	    bit 1: enable/disable the transmitter
	DX = port number
Return: nothing
SeeAlso: AH=0Fh"FOSSIL"
