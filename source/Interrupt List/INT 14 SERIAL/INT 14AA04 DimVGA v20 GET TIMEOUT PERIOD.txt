INT 14 - DimVGA v2.0+ - GET TIME-OUT PERIOD
	AX = AA04h
Return: AX = FFFFh
	BX = current time-out in clock ticks
SeeAlso: AX=AA02h,AX=AA05h,AX=AA0Ah
