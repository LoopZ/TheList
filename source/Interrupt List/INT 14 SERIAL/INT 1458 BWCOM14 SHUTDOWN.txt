INT 14 U - BWCOM14 - SHUTDOWN
	AH = 58h
Return: CX = status (0001h successful, 0002h not initialized)
Note:	after this call, BWCOM14 will no longer redirect the COM port
SeeAlso: AH=56h,AH=57h
