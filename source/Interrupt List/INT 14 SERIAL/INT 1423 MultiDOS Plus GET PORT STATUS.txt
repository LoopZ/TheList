INT 14 - MultiDOS Plus - GET PORT STATUS
	AH = 23h
	DX = port number
Return: AH = line status (see #00304 at AH=03h)
	AL = modem status (see #00305 at AH=03h)
SeeAlso: AH=03h,AH=07h"MultiDOS",AH=20h"MultiDOS"
