INT 1F U - C&T "SuperState" BIOS - GET ALARM DATE
	AH = FCh
	BL = 04h
Return: CH = year century by BCD
	CL = year low by BCD
	DH = month by BCD
	DL = date by BCD
SeeAlso: AH=FCh/BL=02h,AH=FCh/BL=05h
