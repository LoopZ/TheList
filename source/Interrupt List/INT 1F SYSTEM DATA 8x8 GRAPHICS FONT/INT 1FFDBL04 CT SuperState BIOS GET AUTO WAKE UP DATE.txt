INT 1F U - C&T "SuperState" BIOS - GET AUTO WAKE UP DATE
	AH = FDh
	BL = 04h
Return: CH = year century in BCD
	CL = year low in BCD
	DH = month in BCD
	DL = date in BCD
SeeAlso: AH=FDh/BL=02h,AH=FDh/BL=05h
