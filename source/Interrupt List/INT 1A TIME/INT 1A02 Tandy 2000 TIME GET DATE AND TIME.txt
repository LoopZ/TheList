INT 1A - Tandy 2000 - TIME - GET DATE AND TIME
	AH = 02h
Return: BX = number of days since January 1, 1980
	CH = hours
	CL = minutes
	DH = seconds
	DL = hundredths
SeeAlso: AH=03h"Tandy 2000",INT 55"Tandy 2000"
