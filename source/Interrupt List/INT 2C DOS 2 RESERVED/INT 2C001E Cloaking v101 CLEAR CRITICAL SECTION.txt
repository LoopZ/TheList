INT 2C P - Cloaking v1.01 - CLEAR CRITICAL SECTION
	AX = 001Eh
Return: CF clear
Desc:	allow MS Windows to switch to another VM after having prevented it
	  by invoking a critical section
SeeAlso: AX=001Fh,INT 15/AX=101Ch,INT 2F/AX=1682h
