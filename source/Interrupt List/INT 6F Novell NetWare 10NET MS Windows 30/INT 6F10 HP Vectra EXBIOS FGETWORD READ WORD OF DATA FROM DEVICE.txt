INT 6F - HP Vectra EX-BIOS - "F_GET_WORD" - READ WORD OF DATA FROM DEVICE
	AH = 10h
	BP = driver ID (see #03741)
Return: AH = status (see #03744 at AH=00h"HP Vectra")
	DX = data word
	BP,DS destroyed
SeeAlso: AH=08h"F_GET_BYTE",AH=0Ch"F_GET_BUFFER",AH=0Eh"F_PUT_WORD"
