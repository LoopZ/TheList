INT 10 - VIDEO - TEXT-MODE CHARGEN - LOAD ROM MONOCHROME PATTERNS (PS,EGA,VGA)
	AX = 1111h
	BL = block to load
Return: nothing
Notes:	(see AX=1110h)
	the "monochrome" patters are 8x14 pixels in size
SeeAlso: AX=1101h,AX=1110h,AX=1112h,AX=1114h,AH=1Bh,AX=CD10h
