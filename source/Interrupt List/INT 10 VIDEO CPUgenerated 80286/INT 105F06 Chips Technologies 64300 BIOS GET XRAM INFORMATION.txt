INT 10 - Chips & Technologies '64300' BIOS - GET XRAM INFORMATION
	AX = 5F06h
Return: AL = 5Fh if function supported
	    AH = status
		00h unsuccessful
		01h successful
		    BL = XRAM presence (00h no XRAM, 01h present)
SeeAlso: AX=5F05h,AX=5F08h
