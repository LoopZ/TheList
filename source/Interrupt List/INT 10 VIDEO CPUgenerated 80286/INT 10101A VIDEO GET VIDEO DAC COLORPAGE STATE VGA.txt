INT 10 - VIDEO - GET VIDEO DAC COLOR-PAGE STATE (VGA)
	AX = 101Ah
Return: BL = paging mode
	    00h four pages of 64
	    01h sixteen pages of 16
	BH = current page
SeeAlso: AX=1013h
