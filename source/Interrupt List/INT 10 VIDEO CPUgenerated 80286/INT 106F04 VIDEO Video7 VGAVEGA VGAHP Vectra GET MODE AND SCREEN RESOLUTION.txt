INT 10 - VIDEO - Video7 VGA,VEGA VGA,HP Vectra - GET MODE AND SCREEN RESOLUTION
	AX = 6F04h
Return: AL = current video mode (see #00191)
	BX = horizontal columns (text) or pixels (graphics)
	CX = vertical columns (text) or pixels (graphics)
SeeAlso: AX=6F05h
