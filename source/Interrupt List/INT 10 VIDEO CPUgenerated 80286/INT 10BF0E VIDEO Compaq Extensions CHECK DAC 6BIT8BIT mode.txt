INT 10 - VIDEO - Compaq Extensions - CHECK DAC 6-BIT/8-BIT mode
	AX = BF0Eh
Return: CF clear
	AL = DAC mode
	    00h in 6-bit mode
	    01h in 8-bit mode
Note:	supported by at least the QVision board and the SystemPro XL ROM BIOS
SeeAlso: AX=BF0Ch,AX=BF0Dh
