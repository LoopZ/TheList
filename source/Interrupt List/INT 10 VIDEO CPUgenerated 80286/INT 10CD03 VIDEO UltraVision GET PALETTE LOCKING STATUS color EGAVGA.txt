INT 10 - VIDEO - UltraVision - GET PALETTE LOCKING STATUS (color EGA,VGA)
	AX = CD03h
Return: CL = palette locking value
	    00h none
	    01h text modes only
	    FFh all modes
SeeAlso: AX=CD01h
