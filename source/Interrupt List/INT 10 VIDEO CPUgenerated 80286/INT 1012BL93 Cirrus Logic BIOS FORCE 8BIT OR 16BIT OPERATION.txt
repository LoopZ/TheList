INT 10 - Cirrus Logic BIOS - FORCE 8-BIT OR 16-BIT OPERATION
	AH = 12h
	BL = 93h
	AL = new I/O width (00h = 16 bits, 01h = 8 bits)
Return: nothing
SeeAlso: AH=12h/BL=9Ah
