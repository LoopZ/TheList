INT 10 - Cirrus Logic BIOS v3.02 - POWER CONSERVATION
	AH = 12h
	BL = 94h
	AL = new state (00h wake up monitor, 01h shut down display)
Return: nothing
Note:	AL=01h is reported not to work properly on the LCD panel
SeeAlso: AH=12h/BL=84h,AH=12h/BL=92h
