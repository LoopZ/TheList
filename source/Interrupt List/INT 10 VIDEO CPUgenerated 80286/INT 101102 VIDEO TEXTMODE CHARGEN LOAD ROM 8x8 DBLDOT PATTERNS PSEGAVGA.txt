INT 10 - VIDEO - TEXT-MODE CHARGEN - LOAD ROM 8x8 DBL-DOT PATTERNS (PS,EGA,VGA)
	AX = 1102h
	BL = block to load
Return: nothing
Notes:	(see AX=1100h)
SeeAlso: AX=1100h,AX=1101h,AX=1103h,AX=1104h,AX=1112h,AH=1Bh,AX=CD10h
SeeAlso: MEM 0040h:0084h
