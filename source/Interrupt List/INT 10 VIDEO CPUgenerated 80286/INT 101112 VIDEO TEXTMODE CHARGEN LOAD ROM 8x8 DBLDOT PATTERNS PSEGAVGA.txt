INT 10 - VIDEO - TEXT-MODE CHARGEN - LOAD ROM 8x8 DBL-DOT PATTERNS (PS,EGA,VGA)
	AX = 1112h
	BL = block to load
Return: nothing
Notes:	(see AX=1110h)
SeeAlso: AX=1103h,AX=1110h,AX=1111h,AX=1114h,AH=1Bh,AX=CD10h
