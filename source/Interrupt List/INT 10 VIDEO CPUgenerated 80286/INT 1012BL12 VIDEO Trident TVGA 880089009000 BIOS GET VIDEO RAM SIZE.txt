INT 10 - VIDEO - Trident TVGA 8800/8900/9000 BIOS - GET VIDEO RAM SIZE
	AH = 12h
	BL = 12h
Return: AL = 12h if function supported
	    AH = number of 256K banks of RAM installed
		  (read from CRTC register 1Fh)
SeeAlso: AH=12h/BL=11h"TRIDENT"
