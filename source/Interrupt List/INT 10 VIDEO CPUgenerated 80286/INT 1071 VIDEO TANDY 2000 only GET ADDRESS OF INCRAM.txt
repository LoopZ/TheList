INT 10 - VIDEO - TANDY 2000 only - GET ADDRESS OF INCRAM
	AH = 71h
Return: AX:BX -> WORD containing segment address of INCRAM
	AX:CX -> WORD containing offset of INCRAM
SeeAlso: AH=70h"TANDY"
