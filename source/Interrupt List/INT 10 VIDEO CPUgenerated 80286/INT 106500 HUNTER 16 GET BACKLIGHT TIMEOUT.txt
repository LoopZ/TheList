INT 10 - HUNTER 16 - GET BACKLIGHT TIMEOUT
	AX = 6500h
Return: BX = timeout in seconds (0-300)
Desc:	returns the time after which the backlight will turn off
SeeAlso: AH=63h,AH=64h,AX=6501h,INT 15/AH=74h
