INT 10 - VIDEO - XGA - GET SVGA DMQS DATA LENGTH
	AX = 1F02h
Return: AL = 1Fh if function supported
	    BX = length of SVGA DMQS data in bytes
SeeAlso: AX=1F00h,AX=1F03h
