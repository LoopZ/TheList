INT 10 - VIDEO - UltraVision - DISABLE EXTENSIONS
	AX = CC01h
Return: nothing
Notes:	subsequent BIOS calls will be passed through to previous handler
	should be followed immediately by mode set to restore normal EGA/VGA
	  state
SeeAlso: AX=CC02h
