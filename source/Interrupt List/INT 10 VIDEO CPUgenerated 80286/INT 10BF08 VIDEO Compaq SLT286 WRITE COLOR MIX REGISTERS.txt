INT 10 - VIDEO - Compaq SLT/286 - WRITE COLOR MIX REGISTERS
	AX = BF08h
	CH = bits 7-4 green weight
	     bits 3-0 blue weight
	CL = bits 7-4 unused
	     bits 3-0 red weight
Return: nothing
SeeAlso: AH=12h/BL=33h,AX=BF07h
