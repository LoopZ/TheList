INT 10 - VIDEO - Video7 VGA,VEGA VGA - INSTALLATION CHECK
	AX = 6F00h
	BX = 0000h
Return: BX = 5637h ('V7') indicates Video7 VGA/VEGA VGA extensions are present
SeeAlso: AX=6F01h,AX=6F02h,AX=6F03h,AX=6F04h,AX=6F05h
