INT 10 - Cirrus Logic BIOS - GET HIGH REFRESH
	AH = 12h
	BL = AEh
Return:	AL = result
	    bits 7-1: reserved
	    bit 0:    640x480 high refresh rate
		      =0 72 Hz
		      =1 75 Hz
SeeAlso: AH=12h/BL=9Ah,AH=12h/BL=A3h,AH=12h/BL=A4h,AH=12h/BL=AFh
