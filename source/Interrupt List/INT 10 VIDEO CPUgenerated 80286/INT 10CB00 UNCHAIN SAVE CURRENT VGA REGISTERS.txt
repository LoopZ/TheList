INT 10 - UNCHAIN - SAVE CURRENT VGA REGISTERS
	AX = CB00h
Note:	combined with AX=CB06h, this function permits the use of video mode
	  13h together with the VGA's unchained mode
SeeAlso: AX=CB06h,AX=CBFFh
