INT 10 - VIDEO - Hercules GRAFIX - "DISP" - SELECT PAGE TO DISPLAY
	AH = 45h
	AL = page number (0,1)
Return: nothing
Desc:	specify which of the two pages of video memory is visible on screen
SeeAlso: AH=05h,AH=42h,AH=43h
