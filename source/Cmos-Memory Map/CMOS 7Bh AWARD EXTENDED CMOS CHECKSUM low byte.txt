CMOS 7Bh - AWARD - EXTENDED CMOS CHECKSUM (low byte)
Note:	Award's extended checksum is the arithmetic sum of all the bytes
	  from 40h (64 decimal) through 79h (121 decimal). [42h-79h for v4.50G]
SeeAlso: CMOS 7Ah"AWARD"
