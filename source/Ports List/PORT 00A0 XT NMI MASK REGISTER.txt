PORT 00A0 - XT - NMI MASK REGISTER
SeeAlso: PORT 0070h,INT 02

00A0  RW  NMI mask register (XT only)
	 bit 7 = 0 NMI signal disabled from reaching CPU
	       = 1 NMI signal enabled
