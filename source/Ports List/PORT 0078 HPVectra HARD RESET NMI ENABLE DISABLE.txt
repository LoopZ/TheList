PORT 0078 - HP-Vectra - HARD RESET: NMI ENABLE/DISABLE

0078  ?W  NMI enable/disable
		bit 7 = 0  disable & clear hard reset from HP-HIL controller
		      = 1  enable hard reset from HP-HIL controller chip
		bit 6-0	   reserved
